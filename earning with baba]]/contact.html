Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response? +

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

What information should I include in my support request? +

Please include your username, the task ID (if applicable), a clear description of the issue, and any relevant screenshots to help us resolve your query faster.

Do you offer phone support? +

Yes, we offer phone support during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM). For complex issues, we may request additional information via email.

How can I track my support ticket? +

After submitting your inquiry, you'll receive a confirmation email with a ticket number. You can reply to that email thread for updates on your request.
